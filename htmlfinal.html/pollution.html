Pollution

Pollution is one of the biggest problems with cars. Cars emit a variety of pollutants, including carbon dioxide, nitrogen oxides, and particulate matter. These pollutants can have a significant impact on air quality, and can contribute to a variety of health problems, including respiratory problems, heart disease, and cancer.

In addition, cars are a major contributor to climate change. The burning of fossil fuels in cars releases carbon dioxide into the atmosphere, which is a major greenhouse gas. This contributes to global warming, which can have a variety of negative impacts on the environment, including rising sea levels, more frequent and severe weather events, and changes in ecosystems.

Electric cars are just as worse, requiring lithium farming which is incredibly environmentally damaging.
